2. Find all *th* in the text.
3. Calculate mean of given numbers.

6. Calculate _1<sup>2</sup>-2<sup>2</sup>+3<sup>2</sup>-4<sup>2</sup>+...+(-1)<sup>n+1</sup>\*n<sup>2</sup>_.
